Control Simulations

1. Basic Pendulum

Simulates a simple pendulum. Allows real-time manipulation of length and mass, and displays interactive angular response.

2. Inverted Pendulum (PID)

An inverted pendulum stabilized by a custom PID controller. Challenge the controller with mouse disturbances and tune parameters in real-time.

3. LQR Control (Coming Soon)

Linear-Quadratic Regulator applied to the inverted pendulum and cart-pole systems for optimal state feedback.

4. Model Predictive Control

Deploy multi-step predictive algorithms to handle tight constraints and complex environments.

5. Missile Tracking

Proportional navigation and complex guided trajectory simulations.
